INSTALLATION
-------------
1. Copy foaf.module to your modules/ directory
2. Enable foaf.module at administer -> modules
3. Go to administer -> settings -> foaf and complete the field mappings

Note: to take full advantage of the field mappings, you must have
profile.module enabled and configured with appropriate fields made public.
